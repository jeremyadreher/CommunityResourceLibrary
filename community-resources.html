COMMUNITY RESOURCES

Bus Pass
Bus passes are provided by the Human Services Council, if you qualify, by referral only. If you are in need, come to the front desk at WorkSource and request an application. Before the application can be submitted, you will need to meet with an employment specialist. To get assistance with a bus pass, you will be expected to keep a job log of four (4) jobs searches a week, for a total of sixteen (16). In order to receive a bus pass for the current month, you will need to submit your application by the 10th.
